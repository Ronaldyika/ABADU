About us

ABADU (ABASSAKOM DEVELOPMENT UNION) is a political, secular, non-confessional organization with the following objectives:

To foster the socio-economic and cultural development of Fundong sub-division.

To cooperate with the administration in promoting all initiatives that are legally recognized for the development of Fundong sub-division.

About

To educate Abassakom masses in collaboration with the village development union on all socio-cultural and economic practices that are likely to improve the standards of living of Abassakom people.
