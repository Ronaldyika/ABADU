About Us

ABADU (ABASSAKOM DEVELOPMENT UNION) is a political, secular,organization with the following objectives:

-To foster the socio-economic and cultural development of Fundong sub-division.

-To cooperate with the administration in promoting all initiatives that are legally recognized for the development of Fundong sub-division.

-To provide a forum for the come-together of all schools of thoughts of Abassakom in Fundong sub-division.

-To act as an umbrella organization over all village development unions with the acronym VDUs within her area of jurisdiction.

-The Association is open to affiliating to other Associations with the same objectives when needs arise. ABADU seeks financial or material assistance when needs

arise from government and non-governmental organizations within and outside Cameroon to facilitate its developmental projects.

Action Plan

EDUCATION & PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

A large number of Abassakom children lack the basic needs required for a successful learning experience at all levels of education. The lack of financial resources prevents some of our most talented young men and women from pursuing higher education and other advanced professional opportunities It is also evident that schools in remote rural communities are mostly underserved and understaffed, lack the infrastructure and basic material needed for an effective and rewarding teaching/learning process.
